# Roma Candlelight

A cinematic Italian trattoria website with immersive menu browsing, animated reservations, family heritage storytelling, gallery, reviews, maps, and an evening dining mode.

### Original Prompt

> A cinematic Italian restaurant website that feels like stepping into a candlelit trattoria in Rome: warm terracotta, deep olive, cream, and wine-red palette, with elegant serif typography and subtle parchment textures.

- A full-screen hero with slow-motion pasta plating or wood-fired pizza footage, paired with a glowing “Reserve a Table” CTA and ambient accordion/jazz-inspired microinteractions.
- An immersive menu experience with illustrated category tabs, dish cards featuring ingredients, spice/dietary icons, wine pairings, and hover reveals for chef notes and regional origins.
- A live reservations system with table-selection view, peak-time indicators, special occasion add-ons, and instant confirmation animations.
- Story-driven sections for the chef, family heritage, and imported ingredients, plus a gallery styled like a vintage Italian postcard album.
- Delightful touches: floating steam effects, scroll-triggered fade-ins, mobile sticky booking bar, Google Maps/integrated directions, reviews carousel, and a dark “evening dining” mode.
